About

Gymnastics has always been more than routines and scores.
It’s carpools and chalky hugs.
It’s parents in the stands learning the language.
It’s athletes chasing skills that take months — sometimes years — to land.
The sport is built in tenths.
So is the journey.
Other Tenths was created to support both sides of that experience — the athlete working toward their next skill and the parent trying to
understand how to best support them
Because gymnastics isn’t just physical.
It’s technical. Emotional. Incremental. Precise.
And progress often lives in the smallest margins.
Inside Other Tenths, we’re building tools and resources designed specifically for this community:
- An AI cheat sheet to translate skills, scoring, and training into language that makes sense
- A calculator tool to understand how tenths impact scores and meet outcomes
- Individual video critiques packages that offer thoughtful, constructive feedback
- A growing library of gymnastics-centered education and support
This isn’t about pressure.
It’s about clarity.
It’s about helping athletes feel empowered in their progress.
Helping parents feel informed instead of confused.
And creating a space where improvement feels supported — not isolating.
Gymnastics teaches patience.
It teaches resilience.
It teaches that mastery is built in fractions.
Other Tenths exists to walk alongside that process — together.
Because when the community understands the tenths,
the journey becomes stronger for everyone.
